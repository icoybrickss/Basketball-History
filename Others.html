   The National Basketball Association (NBA) is a professional basketball league in North America, widely regarded as one of the premier basketball leagues in the world. Founded in 1946, the NBA consists of 30 teams, divided into two conferences (Eastern and Western) and further subdivided into three divisions each. The league's regular season runs from October to April, with teams competing for playoff berths based on their win-loss records. The playoffs, held in a best-of-seven series format, culminate in the NBA Finals, where the champions of the Eastern and Western Conferences face off for the coveted Larry O'Brien Championship Trophy. The NBA has produced legendary players, iconic moments, and has a global fan base, making it a significant force in the sports and entertainment industry.
